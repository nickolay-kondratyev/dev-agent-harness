Added "WHY-NOT: Eliminating Phase 1" section to doc/core/agent-to-server-communication-protocol.md.

Key points documented:
- /started fires in seconds; first /done can take 30+ minutes for complex tasks
- Two phases provide precise state observability: noStartupAckTimeout (3 min) vs noActivityTimeout (30 min)
- Single-phase collapse would make startup failure detection 10x slower (3 min → 30 min)
- Cannot distinguish "agent crashed on startup" from "agent working on a long task" without Phase 1
