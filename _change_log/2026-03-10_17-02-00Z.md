## What
Added `sealed interface Environment` with `val isTest: Boolean` under `core/initializer/data/`. Two internal implementations: `ProductionEnvironment` (isTest=false) and `TestEnvironment` (isTest=true).

## Changes
- NEW: `app/src/main/kotlin/com/glassthought/chainsaw/core/initializer/data/Environment.kt`
- MODIFIED: `Initializer.initialize()` now accepts `environment: Environment = Environment.production()`
- NEW: `EnvironmentTest.kt` with BDD unit tests
