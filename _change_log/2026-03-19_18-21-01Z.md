## Design decisions
- **Factory lambda for AllSessionsKiller**: Uses `(OutFactory) -> AllSessionsKiller` to decouple from ShepherdContext in tests.
- **Pure create()**: No side effects — caller calls `result.interruptHandler.install()` explicitly.
- **Interim result type**: `TicketShepherdCreatorResult` returned (TicketShepherd class not yet implemented).
- **5 unit tests** verifying wiring correctness with BDD GIVEN/WHEN/THEN structure.
