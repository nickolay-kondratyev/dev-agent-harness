### Rationale
`.ai_out/` directories are always created at initialization (ref.ap.BXQlLDTec7cVVOrzXWfR7.E). If a try ran far enough to matter, its `.ai_out/` directory exists. Branch-only existence (without .ai_out/) means the try never initialized — no conflict possible.
