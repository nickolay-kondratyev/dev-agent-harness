## What changed

Updated spec to capture the decision to keep the `SubPartRole` enum rather than deriving role from sub-part position index.

### Files changed
- `doc/core/SessionsState.md` — expanded SubPartRole description with two-part rationale:
  1. **Self-documentation**: named role concept vs implicit positional convention
  2. **Evolvability**: future roles (FIXER, etc.) are additive enum variants; not building for them now but door is propped open
- `doc/high-level.md` — added "Sub-part role tracking" row to Key Technology Decisions table

### Why
Ticket feedback identified SubPartRole as a simplification candidate (role is deterministic from position). Decision was made to keep it explicit. This commit captures that decision in the authoritative spec.
