### What Changed

**Before:** SetupPlanUseCase returned `SetupPlanResult` sealed class with two variants:
- `Ready(parts)` — static parts
- `NeedsPlanning(planningExecutor, convertPlanToExecutionParts)` — caller had to orchestrate two steps

**After:** `suspend fun setup(): List<Part>` — caller gets execution-ready parts regardless of workflow mode.

### Files Modified
1. `doc/use-case/SetupPlanUseCase/__this.md` — simplified interface, removed SetupPlanResult
2. `doc/use-case/SetupPlanUseCase/DetailedPlanningUseCase.md` — now owns full planning lifecycle
3. `doc/core/TicketShepherd.md` — removed steps 3a-3e, simplified workflow

### Why
- Caller could misuse two-phase protocol (call convertPlan without running executor)
- Planning lifecycle is single responsibility of DetailedPlanningUseCase
- Simpler TicketShepherd workflow (4 steps instead of 5)
