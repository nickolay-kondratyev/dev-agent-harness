## Changes
- New `AgentRequestHandler` interface with 4 suspend methods (onDone, onQuestion, onFailed, onStatus)
- `NoOpAgentRequestHandler` placeholder (no-op, used until phase runner wires real logic)
- `KtorHarnessServer` now injects `AgentRequestHandler`; /question returns handler answer in `{"answer":"..."}`
- `handleAgentRequest` helper updated with action lambda for flexible response body
- Tests: `RecordingAgentRequestHandler` verifies handler invocation and /question response body
