## What changed

The rejection negotiation flow in the granular feedback loop was simplified from 2 disagreement rounds to 1.

**Before:** Doer rejects → reviewer judges → if insist, back to doer (round 1) → if doer rejects again, back to reviewer (round 2) → if reviewer insists again, force doer. Required a loop and disagreementRound counter.

**After:** Doer rejects → reviewer judges → if reviewer accepts, done. If reviewer insists, doer MUST comply immediately. No loop, no counter.

- `doc/plan/granular-feedback-loop.md` (D7, pseudocode, R5, Gate 4, risks)

## Why
- Removes an entire nested loop level from the deepest nesting point
- Fewer states, fewer race conditions, less context window consumption
- Reviewer was already authority — second round just delayed the inevitable
