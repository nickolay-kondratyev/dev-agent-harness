Spec-only change in doc/core/TicketShepherd.md.\n\nOld behavior: first Ctrl+C blocks on stdin for 10 seconds asking y/N.\nNew behavior: first Ctrl+C prints reminder and records timestamp; second Ctrl+C within 2 seconds terminates; after 2s the counter resets.\n\nRationale documented in spec: no stdin contention with StdinUserQuestionHandler, no 10s blocking window, standard CLI idiom users already know.
