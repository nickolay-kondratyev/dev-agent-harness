## Summary

All guard points in PartExecutor previously used a 'one retry via ReInstructAndAwait' pattern.
This simplification removes all retries — guard check failure → immediate AgentCrashed.

## Rationale

The Payload Delivery ACK protocol (ref.ap.tbtBcVN2iCl1xfHJthllP.E) already confirms the
agent received and acknowledged instructions before signaling done. Non-compliance after
ACK-confirmed delivery = broken agent. Retrying wastes time and masks the real problem.
