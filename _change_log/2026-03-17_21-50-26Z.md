## What
Introduced `ReInstructAndAwait` (ap.QZYYZ2gTi1D2SQ5IYxOU6.E) as a spec-level abstraction that encapsulates the repeated "send re-instruction to existing agent session, await next signal" pattern found in 7+ places throughout PartExecutorImpl.

## Why
Each call site previously hand-rolled ~15-20 lines of identical plumbing: fresh deferred creation, SessionEntry re-registration, AckedPayloadSender delivery, health-aware await loop, crash propagation. The abstraction collapses this into one call with a clean `ReInstructOutcome` sealed class (`Responded`, `FailedWorkflow`, `Crashed`).
