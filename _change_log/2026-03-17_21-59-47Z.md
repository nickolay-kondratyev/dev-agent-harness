Added ProcessResult data class. TmuxCommandRunner.run() now captures stdout and stderr concurrently (avoids buffer-full deadlock) and returns ProcessResult instead of Int.

All callers (TmuxCommunicatorImpl, TmuxSessionManager) updated to use result.exitCode and include result.stdErr in exception messages, giving actionable diagnostic info on tmux failures.
