## Changes\n\n- **doc/core/SessionsState.md**: Removed `subPartRole: SubPartRole` field from SessionEntry table. Added `subPartIndex: Int` — position in the sub-parts array. Role is now derived on-the-fly via `SubPartRole.fromIndex(subPartIndex)` (0=DOER, 1=REVIEWER). Removed the lengthy 'Why an enum / Why NOT derive from position' justification (now obsolete).\n- **doc/schema/plan-and-current-state.md**: No changes needed — session record schema never stored subPartRole.\n\n## Rationale\n\nSubPartRole was redundant with array position. Single source of truth in `fromIndex()` eliminates divergence risk and simplifies the schema.
