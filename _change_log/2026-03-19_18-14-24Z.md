## What changed
- Deleted `PendingQuestion.kt` (redundant wrapper)
- Deleted `core/session/UserQuestionContext.kt` (identical duplicate)
- Updated `SessionEntry.questionQueue` type to `ConcurrentLinkedQueue<UserQuestionContext>`
- Updated `SessionEntryTest` to construct `UserQuestionContext` directly
- Updated `doc/core/SessionsState.md` to reflect simplified structure
- Added spec AP cross-reference to canonical `UserQuestionContext`

## Why
DRY violation: `PendingQuestion.question` was never independently accessed — it always duplicated `context.question`. Two identical `UserQuestionContext` classes in different packages added confusion about canonical import.
