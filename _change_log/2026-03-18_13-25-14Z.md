## Change\nRemoved the bounded retry loop (maxRecoveryAttempts=2) from AutoRecoveryByAgentUseCase spec.\n\nThe use case now runs the recovery agent exactly once:\n- Success → Resolved\n- Escalation marker → NeedsEscalation\n- Failed/TimedOut → Unresolvable\n\n## Rationale\nA second spawn receives identical context and has no reason to succeed where the first failed. Worst-case recovery time drops from 40 min to 20 min before human escalation.\n\n## Files changed\n- doc/use-case/AutoRecoveryByAgentUseCase.md
