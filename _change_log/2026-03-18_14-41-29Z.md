## What Changed

Health pings previously had a dedicated `/signal/ping-ack` endpoint and `callback_shepherd.signal.sh ping-ack` command. Now pings are wrapped in the same `<payload_from_shepherd_must_ack>` XML with PayloadId and use the generic `/signal/ack-payload` endpoint.
