## Changes
- `AppDependencies` changed from `data class` to `class` implementing `AsgardCloseable`
- `httpClient` created in `initialize()`, stored in `AppDependencies`, passed to `createGLMDirectLLM()`
- `close()` shuts down dispatcher executor, evicts connection pool, and closes `outFactory`
- `App.kt` uses `deps.use {}` instead of `deps.outFactory.use {}` — all resources now closed on shutdown
- Added unit test `AppDependenciesCloseTest` verifying executor shutdown after `close()`
