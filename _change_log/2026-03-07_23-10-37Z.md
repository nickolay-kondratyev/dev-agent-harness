## What
Created root `build.gradle.kts` applying the Gradle `idea` plugin.

## Why
The `submodules/thorg-root` submodule is large and was being indexed by IntelliJ, slowing down the IDE. Only `source/libraries/kotlin-mp` (the composite build dependency) needs to be accessible.

## How
Three-level drill-down exclusion:
1. Exclude all dirs directly under `thorg-root/` except `source/`
2. Exclude all dirs under `source/` except `libraries/`
3. Exclude all dirs under `source/libraries/` except `kotlin-mp/`

## Note
Individual files at `thorg-root/` root level (scripts, markdown) cannot be excluded — `excludeDirs` only accepts directories. This is expected behavior.
