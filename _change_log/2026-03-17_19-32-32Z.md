## What Changed

The WHY-NOT Comments Protocol (ap.kmiKk7vECiNSpJjAXYMyE.E) was a full sub-protocol specifying:
- A specific `// WHY-NOT(YYYY-MM-DD):` comment format
- Three trigger sources (reviewer→doer, doer pushback, doer self-discovered)
- Non-immutability semantics
- Reviewer validation of comment placement

This was over-engineering for V1. Replaced with a single bullet point in the doer pushback guidance:
> "Document non-obvious decisions and pitfalls in code comments."
