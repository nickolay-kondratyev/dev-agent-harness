## Clock Abstraction (ref.ap.whDS8M5aD2iggmIjDIgV9.E)

New package: `com.glassthought.shepherd.core.time`

### Files
- `Clock.kt`: Interface + SystemClock (production, delegates to Instant.now())
- `TestClock.kt`: Test double with advance(Duration) and set(Instant)
- `ClockTest.kt`: 7 BDD tests covering SystemClock and TestClock behaviors

### Design Decisions
- Regular interface (not fun interface) since Clock may gain methods later
- TestClock in test sources only — not shipped in production
- Uses kotlin.time.Duration with toJavaDuration() bridge for Instant arithmetic
